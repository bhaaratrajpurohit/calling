⚡ The 10% Buffer — Your Safety Net Against Hidden Costs
Your calculated cost is what you plan to pay. Your actual bill is always higher. The buffer covers that invisible gap between spreadsheet and reality.
  • Failed call attempt fees: Twilio charges ₹1.26 per outbound attempt even when nobody picks up. 100 missed calls = ₹126 gone silently every month.
  • Per-minute rounding: Twilio rounds every call up to the next full minute. A 2 min 10 sec call is billed as 3 full minutes — inflating your actual minutes by 15–20% at scale.
  • LLM token overruns: When leads ask unexpected questions, the AI burns extra tokens. Your AI platform bill quietly spikes above the base rate.
  • Re-dials and retries: A dropped call mid-conversation triggers a retry — double billing on telephony AND AI for that call.
  • Voicemail detection time: The AI stays on the line 10–15 seconds listening for voicemail tones. That time is billed. 200 voicemails/month ≈ 40 extra billed minutes.
  • Mid-month platform surcharges: Some AI platforms silently add surcharges or update rates without prominent notice. Buffer absorbs this.
→ 10% is conservative for real estate calling. Adjust it from 5% to 25% using the slider below based on your risk tolerance.
